What each version does well

v1 strength. Pointing. If someone asks "what's in llm-wiki?" you can literally point at the frame. It's the right metaphor for planning-mode — reviewing goals, auditing backlogs.

v2 strength. Relationships. You see llm-wiki edge-connected to r11 because both the aggregator and the participant are part of the same field. It's the right metaphor for discovery-mode — noticing unexpected adjacencies.
